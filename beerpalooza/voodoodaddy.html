Big Black Voodoo Daddy

Voodoo Brewing Co.

The name says it all. BBVD is a big bad seasonal stout made with copious amounts of chocolate and roasted malts and topped off with the right amount of hops to make it taste like a chocolate bar au natural. Good to drink now or age it to award winning caliber.

The Employee Owners of Voodoo Brewing Co. have taken their brewing experiences and quirky personalities and wrapped it up into a line of beers oriented around what we feel are fun, flavorful, and thought-provoking.
